ABOUT US

Welcome to our website!

We are Olena Litovska, Viktoria Kryshesvka, and Anastasia Hileta, students of group KN-201.

We are delighted to present to you our calculation and graphic work in the form of a captivating web site, a musical online shop “Bass & Treble”, which includes this very page..

Our journey began with a shared vision to provide music lovers with a convenient platform to explore and purchase their favorite musical instruments.

With our combined skills and dedication, we have meticulously crafted a web-based solution that caters to the needs of musicians of all levels.

As authors of the calculation and graphic work behind this website, we have strived to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface. Our goal is to offer a seamless browsing experience, allowing visitors to easily navigate through a vast selection of musical instruments, accessories, and equipment.

At our online shop, you will find a wide range of instruments, from guitars and keyboards to drums. We have carefully curated our collection to ensure high-quality products from renowned brands. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned musician, we have something to suit your taste and budget.
